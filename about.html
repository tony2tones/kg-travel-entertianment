The Visionary

Kgomotso Moshapo

Founder & Luxury Experience Curator

Born on 29 July 1990 in Limpopo, Kgomotso Moshapo spent over 20 years in Johannesburg before relocating to Cape Town in 2020.

What began as a fresh start quickly became a love story with the city.

From breathtaking ocean views and iconic mountains to vibrant nightlife, wine farms and adventure experiences — Cape Town became more than home. It became a lifestyle.

Always exploring and discovering hidden gems, Kgomotso realised his passion for curating unforgettable experiences could become something greater.

"KG Travel & Entertainment was born from a genuine love for people, premium experiences and showing others what they've been missing."

Fun. Detail-oriented. Luxury-driven.
